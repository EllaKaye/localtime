A Quarto extension, `localtime`, for displaying times in the local timezone. E.g. I’d like to be able to type, say, {{< localtime 2026-01-30 13:00 UTC >}} and have that appear as 2026-01-30 14:00 for someone reading the page in France, and 2026-01-30 8:00 for someone reading the page in Toronto. Or {{< localtime 2026-01-30 13:00 EST >}} appears as 2026-01-30 10:00 for someone in PST.
